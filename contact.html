About Us

Legon TV is more than just a student platform — it’s a vibrant media outlet that captures stories from across Ghana and beyond, focusing on what matters most to students, youth, and the academic world. From campus news to entertainment, lifestyle, education trends, and global events, Legon TV delivers fresh and engaging content that informs, inspires, and connects.

Whether you're into politics, fashion, sports, or culture, Legon TV brings it all under one roof, powered by young creatives with a vision. We don’t just report stories — we tell them with energy and excellence.
