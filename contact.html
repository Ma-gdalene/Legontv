About LegonTV

LegonTV is your window into campus culture, education, and global student life. From the latest entertainment buzz to political developments that affect students and educators, we bring it all under one roof. With a mission to inform, engage, and inspire, we cover real stories from real people—on and off campus.

Our team is made up of student journalists, content creators, and creatives passionate about uncovering the pulse of university life in Ghana and beyond. We are storytellers for a generation that wants the truth, told with authenticity and energy. LegonTV doesn’t just report the news; we shape the conversation.
