About LegonTV

LegonTV is your premier student-run digital platform bringing campus news, entertainment, and global conversations to your screen. We cover everything from university happenings to music, pop culture, education, innovation, and social trends — all with a student-focused lens.

Our mission is to inform, engage, and entertain. Whether it's highlighting school events, promoting student talent, or analyzing global issues that affect the youth, LegonTV is here to amplify student voices and showcase excellence.

Follow us as we explore campus life, share important updates, and connect students to the wider world. LegonTV isn't just a blog — it's a movement.
